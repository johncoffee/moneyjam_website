When/where

April-May 2014, somewhere in Copenhagen...

What is this?!

Money Jam is a creative challenge where the goal is to make money.

In fact, this is the first-ever Money Jam in the history of money!

If you're familiar with hackathons, Startup Weekends, or game jams, that's how it will go down. We'll kick off the challenge with a cozy jam weekend full of creating, planning, hacking, building, thinking, selling, and buying. After the kick off, there will be a period of a months time where the projects can sell/"do their work".

How to win?

Participants elects the winners in the end. You do not necessarily win by ending up with the biggest amount of money. You'll rather want to impress the others with crazy ideas or some insight that really worked.

Diversifiers / "achievements"

  • Make the most transactions
  • Spend nothing
  • Biggest revenue

I don't get it... Examples?

Again, Money Jam is a creative challenge. You should go for a project where you'll try something new, have fun, aim for uncertainty, and/or take risk.. How about playing with some new technologies, like crypto currencies? Try to sell a game you've made, or put something on the Unity Asset Store?
