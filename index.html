Money Jam

Because playing with money is like playing guitar. Sounds good, doesn't it?
Who doesn't want money? Lots of money! Beautiful shiny money!
If you like Monopoly, you'll love money jam!

Pay attention!

Money Jam is a creative challenge where the goal is to make money.

What's the format

If you're familiar with hackathons or game jams, you know the format of money jam. We kick off the challenge with a cozy jam weekend full of creating, planning, hacking, building, thingking, selling and buying.

Rules:

Everyone donates 50% of what they make to a pool that will be given to the winners.

There will be

Diversifiers / "achievements"

Your team can choose to persue the following. Do it for fun, or do it because it's part of your plan anyway.

  • Make the most transactions
  • Make least transactions, but the most money
  • Trade as many currencies as you can
  • Have the biggest amount of money
  • Have the least amount of money
  • Spend nothing

What does it cost?

Good question :) If you actually make money, then you end up with more money than you started with. But time is money, so no matter what you'll spend the time it takes. Catering will be provided!
